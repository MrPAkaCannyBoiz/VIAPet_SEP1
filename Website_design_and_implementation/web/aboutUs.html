About our shop

Learn about the history of our store.

VIAPets is a shop that has transformed from a passion for animals into a place where every animal lover can find something for themselves. Founded in 996 by a group of veterinarian friends, the shop quickly gained a reputation for its commitment to animal welfare and high-quality customer service. Our animals come from trusted and responsible breeders, and each new home for our animals is carefully selected to provide them with the best possible life.

You will find a variety of animals here, from dogs and cats, through birds, to more exotic animals. We also offer a wide selection of accessories and food, so you can provide your new friend with all the best.

Our priority is the satisfaction of both our clients and our animal charges. That is why we regularly organize training and workshops on animal care, which are very popular among our clients.

Dare to visit VIAPets and join our growing family of animal lovers!
