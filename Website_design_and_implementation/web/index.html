Through Bob's vision ViaPet was born...

Bob’s vision for ViaPet is to create a sanctuary for pets and a trusted partner for pet owners in the community. He believes in providing a caring, safe environment that caters to the needs of every animal and brings convenience to pet owners' lives. ViaPet’s kennel offers a "home away from home" for dogs and cats, where owners can leave their pets with confidence, knowing they’ll receive compassionate care in a secure, comfortable space.

Beyond offering a top-tier kennel service, ViaPet is designed to be a complete resource for all pet needs. The store features a wide array of animals—including birds, rodents, dogs, cats, and fish—so people can find the perfect pet to welcome into their lives. To ensure the well-being of every pet, ViaPet also provides an extensive selection of high-quality products, from nutritious foods and essential health supplies to fun toys and accessories that enrich pets' lives.

Bob is committed to making ViaPet a truly customer-friendly experience. He has built a team that prioritizes warmth, friendliness, and expertise, ensuring every visitor feels welcomed and supported. His vision is rooted in the belief that pet ownership should be joyful and stress-free, and he is dedicated to providing guidance, products, and services that foster positive relationships between people and their pets. Through ViaPet, Bob aims to create a community of happy pets and satisfied pet owners, where everyone feels like family.
